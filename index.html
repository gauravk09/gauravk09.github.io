Signature work

Two-stage LLM support pipeline ~$5.2M / yr

A cheap model summarizes, then a chain-of-thought call classifies team and theme. 100K+ conversations a month at 93% validated accuracy. Cut contact volume 20% and surfaced emerging issues to product teams before they showed up in dashboards. Monitored in three layers: daily LLM-as-judge scoring, weekly embedding clustering of low-confidence outputs, and biweekly human audits holding Cohen's Kappa above 0.70.

Enhanced Due Diligence funnel rebuild ~$8.6M / yr

High-risk users were dropping out of EDD at the email step. Diagnosing that drop-off and moving the flow in-app took completion from 15% to 75% across 4K monthly users. A parallel read on the KYC Refresh funnel lifted completion from 35% to 50% across 500K monthly users, preventing ~$375K in restriction-driven revenue loss.

Difference-in-differences model for CX quality ~$720K / yr

A clean causal read on what a quality support interaction is actually worth in revenue, which is what made the automation-versus-quality tradeoff arguable with numbers instead of instinct. CSAT up 5%.

Asset-recommendation engagement drivers ~$5M / yr

CTR and segment analysis isolating what actually moves engagement: price momentum, new-user behavior, long-tail assets. Turned that read into incremental annualized revenue.
